Baked Beans

Description

Loaded with rich spices, fragrant herbs and a spoonful of maple syrup, these are not your average baked beans. Serve with Brazil nuts and toasted sourdough for a sophisticated take on a comfort food classic.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 red onion, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • large pinch dried oregano
  • 2 x 400g tins butter beans, drained
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp chopped flat-leaf parsley

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan, then add the onion and saut√© for 3-4 mins. Stir in the spices and oregano and cook for 1 min, then stir in the beans.
  2. Add the tomatoes, balsamic glaze, maple syrup and 100ml water; bring to the boil. Simmer for 15 mins.
  3. Season. Stir in the parsley and nuts and serve with sourdough, if you like.
