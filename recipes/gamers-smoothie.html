Gamer's Smoothie

Description

This iconic smoothie requires no garbage foods as fruits or vegetables. Gamer's Smoothie is a %100 vegan drink with high nutrients. It is invented by our lord and savior Gaben himself when he dropped out from Harvard.

If you are spending most of your time on computers for either developing webpages or playing competitive games with 13 years olds, this is the perfect dinner for you. With being able to drink your mountain dew and eating doritos at the same time you will save a lot of AFK time to better focus on what you are doing on a computer.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of doritos
  • 1 lt mountain dew

Steps

  1. Blend doritos and mountain dew in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour into glass and enjoy.
